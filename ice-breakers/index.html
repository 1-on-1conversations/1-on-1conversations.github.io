Ice Breaker Questions

The list of questions below has been compiled to help participants break the ice and to get to know each other:

  1. Tell me about an embarrassing moment in your life.
  2. Tell me about something that you've done that makes you proud.
  3. Before this meetup, when was the last time you had a conversation with a stranger?
  4. Have you ever seen a shooting star? When was the last time?
  5. When was the last time you gave someone flowers?
  6. When was the last time someone gave you flowers?
  7. What's your zodiac sign? Do you believe in the horoscope?
  8. Do you know how to ride a bicycle? How old where you when you learn it?
  9. What's your favorite sport? Do you practice it regularly?
  10. When was the last time you went out of town? Where did you go?
  11. Have you ever been to a circus? When was the last time you went to one?
  12. Have you ever been to a foreign country? When was the last time?
  13. Do you know how to swim? How old were you when you learned it?
  14. Do you have a best friend? How did you meet him
  15. If you could choose to have a super power, what would that be?
  16. What's is your favorite cartoon character?
  17. What's the last movie you've watched at the movie theaters? Why did you choose it?
  18. Do you know how to cook? What's the best dish you can make?
  19. How much water do you drink on a daily basis?
  20. What was your favorite toy as a child?
