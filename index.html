What is 1-on-1 Conversations?

1-on-1 Conversations is a movement. The vision is to create communities (meetup groups) in as many cities as possible, where people can come together to meet and talk to each other. Some communities might have a specific purpose such as language exchange.

What makes it unique?

1-on-1 Conversation meetups follow a specific format that allows everyone to talk as many people as possible on a 1-on-1 basis, for an arbitrary amount of time (i.e. 15 minutes). The meetup host will pair everyone up, start the timer, and conduct rotations when the time is up.

Where do I find one?

Currently there are 1-on-1 Conversation meetups in Phoenix, AZ and in Lima, Peru.
