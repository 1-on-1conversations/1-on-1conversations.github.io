What is 1-on-1 Conversations?

1-on-1 Conversations is a place where people get together to practice language conversation skills on a 1-on-1 basis.

How does it work?

When you show up to your first 1-on-1 Conversation meetup, you'll be greeted by the event host who will introduce you to the rest of the students. The format of the meetup is very simple: you'll be paired up with a person, and you'll have 10 to 15 minutes to talk to that person. Once the time is up, the event host will conduct a rotation, and you'll talk a new person.

Where do I find one?

Currently there are 1-on-1 Conversation meetups in Phoenix, AZ and in Lima, Peru.
